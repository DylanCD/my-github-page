FACET

Facet is a mobile skincare companion app designed to educate users on skincare ingredients and help them make informed decisions based on their personal skin concerns. Facet does not promote or endorse any specific products or brands. Instead, it offers personalized ingredient recommendations tailored to the user's skin needs, age group, and preferences. Whether you're dealing with acne, dryness, sensitivity, or just want to better understand what's in your products, Facet empowers you with knowledge at your fingertips.
